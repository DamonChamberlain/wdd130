Three Great Adventures to Choose From

The Family Fun Float (Class II)

Perfect for families with young children or first-time rafters! Enjoy a peaceful, scenic float on the lower river. This half-day trip is all about relaxation.

  • **Duration:** Half-Day (3 hours)
  • **Best for:** Ages 5+, Beginners
  • **Price:** $59 per person

Browns Canyon Adventure (Class III)

Our most popular full-day trip! Experience the excitement of intermediate rapids as you navigate the famous Browns Canyon. This trip requires teamwork.

  • **Duration:** Full-Day (6 hours, including lunch)
  • **Best for:** Ages 10+, Intermediate
  • **Price:** $129 per person

The Grand Finale Express (Class IV-V)

For the adrenaline junkies only! This is a challenging, full-throttle experience on the upper river with big drops and powerful hydraulics. Prior experience recommended.

  • **Duration:** Half-Day (4 hours)
  • **Best for:** Ages 16+, Expert
  • **Price:** $109 per person
